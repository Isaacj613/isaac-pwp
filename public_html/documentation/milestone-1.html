Purpose

I want to create a website that people can come and view my work and find my contact information.

Audience

The audience I hope to achieve to capture with this website are employers or people interested in my work as well as possible clients.

Goal

  • To acquire a full or part-time job.
  • To gather interest of employers or possible clients.
  • To be used as a display of skills by presenting my work
  • Making it an easy place to get my contact information or social media accounts.
